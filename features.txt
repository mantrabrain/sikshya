Sikshya - WordPress Learning Management System (LMS) Plugin
================================================================

CORE FEATURES
============

1. COURSE MANAGEMENT
   - Create and manage unlimited courses
   - Course categories and tags
   - Course prerequisites and dependencies
   - Course enrollment options (free, paid, subscription)
   - Course completion certificates
   - Course progress tracking
   - Course ratings and reviews
   - Course search and filtering
   - Course templates and duplication

2. LESSON MANAGEMENT
   - Create structured lessons within courses
   - Multiple lesson types (text, video, audio, file, quiz)
   - Lesson prerequisites
   - Lesson duration tracking
   - Lesson completion requirements
   - Lesson attachments and resources
   - Lesson comments and discussions
   - Lesson ordering and sequencing

3. QUIZ & ASSESSMENT SYSTEM

   - Random question selection
